Manufacturing

We have two large PCB manufacturing bases for advanced PCB and one EMS PCBA factory for PCB assembly, all with total manufacturing capacity and experienced engineers and technicians. From PCB prototypes to massive manufacturing, you can handle your project with us and we provide you with optimization and one-on-one engineering support. If you want to see how we produce, assemble, test, and package PCBs, you can have an online meeting with us, where we will show you the factory monitor live stream!

Copper etching in PCB fabrication

We generate circuits on copper sheets by etching away unwanted copper.

Scrubbing and cleaning copper sheets

We scrub the copper sheets to increase roughness and clean them.

Immersion copper

Through a chemical reaction, we deposit copper on the surface of the board or the wall of the PCB holes.

VCP copper plating

We adopt the more advanced vertical continuous plating (VCP) method to plate copper on the wall of PCB holes.

Placing SMDs in SMT assembly

On an SMT line, surface-mount devices are picked and placed automatically on circuit boards.

Metallographic inspection

Under a microscope, we check the PCBA cross-section to ensure good copper filling and circuit connection.

PCB automatic electroplating

We adopt the automatic electroplating method to apply the hard gold plating surface finish on PCBs.

PCBA microscope check

We observe the solder joints under a microscope to ensure no poor welding.
