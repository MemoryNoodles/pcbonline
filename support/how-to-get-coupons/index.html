How to get coupon

There are three ways to get PCBONLINE coupons. One surprise for you is that multiple coupons can be used for on purchase.

There are three ways to get PCBONLINE coupons. One surprise for you is that multiple coupons can be used for on purchase.

1) Register from us for free 
  • If you are a new user, once you register from PCBONLINE, you will immediately get a total of $100 coupons (one $50 coupon, two $20 coupons, and two $5 coupons), which sets no minimum spend limit to your order. The coupons stay valid until the expiration date after three months from the registration moment.
  • Please note that we set the minimum order amount for use of coupons received in this way. If the order amount is within $100, you can use $10 off coupons; order amount from $101 to $499, you can use $50 off coupons; order amount from $500 to $1,000, you can use $100 off coupons.
2) Redeem points for coupons
  • Every time you buy online from PCBONLNE, you earn certain points based on your order amount. 1 dollar of order amount equals to 1 point and the points will be added to your account when your order is reported to us. Below is how many points can be used to redeem coupons.

  • 5,000 points = a $20 coupon

    10,000 points = a $50 coupon

    20,000 points = two $50 coupons + one $20 coupon

    50,000 points = five $50 coupons + five $20 coupons

    100,000 points = twelve $50 coupons +  five $20 coupons

3) Get coupons by promotions
  • Here are several ways to get coupons through different promotions.
  • B. Share our website page https://www.pcbonline.com on your social network site (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkIn, etc.) and @mention PCBONLINE so that we can know your promotion and then give you a $10 coupon
  • C. Share your promotion code to others, and when others copy and paste your code and register from PCBONLINE, you will be rewarded with a $20 coupon.
  • D. You post a blog/article containing any link of our website and with more than 500 words on a topic-related forum or your social new work, and send the proof (the link) to us via email at info@pcbonline.com. When we audit your post, we will give you a $40 coupon.
  • E. Make a review video about PCBONLINE and share the project on your social networks. If the video gains more than 500 views, contact us and you will be refunded with a $50-$200 coupon. 
  • F. Go to our suggestion page and post your suggestion to PCBONLNE. Once your suggestion is implemented, you will be rewarded with a $100-$500 coupon.
  • E. Students can apply to us for free PCB samples via email at info@pcbonline.com.
  • Note: the above promotions apply to online orders only.
