Rigid flex PCB Manufacturing Capabilities

What is Rigid Flex PCB

Rigid-flex PCBs are complex boards using a combination of rigid PCB and flexible PCB technologies. The inner layers of the rigid-flex PCB's rigid section are flex, and the board's rigid section and flex section are electrically interconnected. The entire flex layers are laminated first, next, the rigid layers are laminated externally (the rigid part of the flex section is without circuits), and then the rigid FR4 is removed from the flex section to create the rigid flex PCB.

PCB Fabrication Process and Prices

In regard to the PCB fabrication price, it depends on the PCB fabrication complexity, materials, and PCB quantity.
Generally, more PCB layers and higher copper thickness lead to a higher PCB fabrication cost. FR4 and aluminum PCBs are cheaper than copper-based, PI, PET, PTFE, and ceramic substrate PCBs. And the unit price of large PCB production is lower than that of small and prototype orders.
