Quality Assurance of PCBA

  • • Before batch PCB assembly, we carry out the FAI (first article inspection) to make sure everything is right and generate a report for you to review.
  • • We follow standard SOP to control the temperature and speed of reflow soldering ovens to ensure solder paste filtering and soldering, which is key to the SMT process.
  • • PCBA tests after soldering include ICT (in-circuit test), FCT (functional test), aging test, temperature, and humidity test, drop test, and testing before and after packaging.
  • • During PCB assembly, the PCBA goes through a series of tests, such as SPI (solder paste inspection), X-Ray, and AOI (automated optical inspection), and all results will be recorded and reported to you.
  • • Mould design for wave soldering is operated by experienced project engineers, which ensures smooth DIP assembly.
  • • We shorten the SMT/DIP assembly cycle due to 30,000+ kinds of electronic components in-warehouse, which dramatically saves your time.
